    WHO IS RASEC MARC?

    Rasec Marc is a Filipino-American singer/songwriter, producer, and composer. Fusing emotional lyricism with colorful pop-oriented instrumentation, his music creates a space for listeners to feel understood and empowered. From the heartstring tugs of Hurts to the infectious, love-soaked Conclusions, each song offers both a unique message to connect with and a beautiful world to get lost in. Fresh off of opening for Filipino giants Ben&Ben on their first North American tour, Rasec is poised for a bountiful 2023 with a slew of exciting releases on the horizon.

    Rasec Marc’s producing prowess extends well beyond the confines of his own releases. With numerous production credits working alongside some of the brightest up-and-coming Filipino artists to his name, he has grown into a highly sought-after collaborator amongst his peers. His most recent efforts have seen him work with rising star KLARRENCE, FilAm King of Talk Jules Graeser, and master-balladeer Kenneth Laurente. On the composition front, 2022 saw Rasec serve as music composer for his first ever feature-length film on Howling Gale Entertainment’s Tahanan. He has already started off 2023 with work on a new film, adding to the excitement of the new year.
